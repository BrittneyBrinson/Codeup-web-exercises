Estes Park Colorado

Where is Estes National Park located?
Located 90 miles northwest of Denver, Colorado. Sitting in the heart of the Rocky Mountains
Are there bears in Estes Park?
Yes! the bear population is mostly active during summer and the fall season.
Are there cabins available during the winter season?
Yes! We have many cabins available on a first come, first serve basis.
Will there be medical services for emergencies?
Yes, medical services can called but due to the secluded nature of the park, medical services will be a fair distance away. We have on-site medical 24/7 but for emergencies do have helicopter capabilities.
What activities does the park offer?
We offer horse-back mountain trail tours, fishing classes, and bison/elk viewing tours!
